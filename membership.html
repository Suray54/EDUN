Membership details:

Membership type:

1. General Member Membership Fee: 750 NRS Renewal Fee: 250 NRS
2. Life Member Membership Fee: 5000 NRS
3. Executive Member Membership Fee: 2000 NRS Renewal Fee: 500 NRS

• Personal Contact Information
By providing your email address you will be automatically subscribed to receive the majority of our communications via e-mail. An email address is required for event, volunteer, workshop registrations and membership renewal form. We request personal information to keep you informed of local medical society events you may wish to participate in.. The information you provide in the "personal contact information" section will not be released to any person or entity. However, some events require us to share registration information to sponsors for appropriate follow-up under limited circumstances.

• MEMBERSHIP REQUIREMENTS:
Membership is available to anyone who
1. Holds the citizenship of Nepal
2. Is of 16 years of age or older
3. Is mentally stable
4. Hasn’t done any sort of damage to the country’s public property
5. Hasn’t been charged with any sort of criminal/ corruption activities as per the Constitution of Nepal
6. Works in favor of the organization
7. Hasn’t been convicted of any crime as per the Constitution of Nepal

Membership Benefits: Our impact is greater together than it is when we work individually Among the privileges, benefits and services of being a General/ life/ executive Member of EduN are:
1. Training Workshops with experts on topics as education, innovation, empowerment planning, rapport building, skill development, and other practical issues.
2. Opportunities to team up with other members of the organization to help create our own projects throughout which the organization will provide technical and capacity building assistance, and more.
3. Involvement in planning and execution of the projects to be carried out by the organization.
4. Conferences provide a unique crossroads for the team members to meet, share innovative ideas and practices, and build partnerships that can help the NGOs better fulfill their missions.
5. Symposia and roundtables are also periodically conducted and members will have substantially reduced rates to attend our conferences and symposia.
6. Organization Members will have access to the information on every program/ project to be carried out by the organization beforehand.
7. (General/ life/ executive) Members will have the right to vote for the board members of the organization.
8. (General/ life/ executive) members have the right to propose or nominate themselves to be a part of the committee (either board /executive) which will help us advance the goals of our organization.
9. Full access to the organization’s infrastructures (reading, working materials, etc) resources either through the website or from our office
10. 15-20% discount on all online and face-to-face workshops to General/ life/ executive
11. (…%) discount on various workshops/ leadership training, skill development workshops conducted with our partner organizations.
12. A (certificate/letter of recommendation) recognition will be provided to all the EduN member
